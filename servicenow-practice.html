Our Core ServiceNow Practice Areas

IT Service Management (ITSM)

Transform your IT operations into a centralized, proactive service experience.
  • Incident, Request, Change & Problem Management
  • Employee Self-Service Portals for quick access and submissions
  • Automated Workflows to handle approvals, escalations, and communications
  • Cross-Department Collaboration: Connect IT with HR, Procurement, and Facilities on one platform
  • Knowledge Base & Virtual/Live Agents for 24/7 employee support

Customer Service Management (CSM)

Deliver seamless support experiences that delight customers and vendors alike.
  • Case Management & Routing
  • Agent Workspaces for faster resolution
  • Knowledge Management for self-service and reduced ticket volume
  • External Integrations to connect with vendor/customer platforms
  • Code-less Configuration to rapidly adapt to changing needs
  • Virtual/Live Agents for 24/7 customer/vendor support

Field Service Management (FSM)

Support your field teams and streamline operations from dispatch to delivery.
  • Work Order Creation & Scheduling
  • Auto-Dispatching Based on Technician Availability
  • Inventory & Parts Management with vendor integration
  • Mobile-Enabled NOW Agent App for technicians on the move
  • Invoice Submission & Work Order Closure tracking for vendors
  • Knowledge Base & Virtual/Live Agents for 24/7 support

Give employees a consumer-like experience for HR (People) services — no emails, no confusion.

  • Confidentiality and Compliance with role-based access and case visibility

Procurement Service Management
