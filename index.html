WHO WE ARE

RAMIN IT is an IT Consulting “Services & Solutions” firm specializing in the delivery of Data Management, IT Service Management, Business Analysis, Data Analysis & Business Intelligence to diverse clients across multiple industries.

WHAT WE DO

IT Consulting

A discipline that focuses on advising organizations on how best to use information technology in achieving their business objectives.

IT Services & Solutions

A field of activity that deals with business and technical expertise to enable organizations in the creation, management and optimization of or access to information and business processes.

IT Application Development

A process of creating software programs to address business needs. The process encompasses feasibility analysis, requirements gathering, designing prototypes, testing, implementation and integration.

IT Quality Assurance

A discipline that ensures that an organization delivers the best products or services possible.

WHAT WE OFFER

Business Intelligence

Business intelligence (BI) combines business analytics, data mining, data visualization, data tools and infrastructure, and best practices to help organizations to make more data-driven decisions.

Data Analysis

Data analysis is the process of collecting, modeling and analyzing data to extract insights that support decision-making.

Data Governance

Data Governance is a collection of practices and processes which help to ensure the formal management of data assets within an organization.

Data Quality

Data Quality is a measure of the condition of data based on factors such as accuracy, completeness, consistency, reliability and whether it's up to date.

Master Data Management

Master Data Management (MDM) is a technology-enabled discipline in which business and Information Technology work in unison to ensure the uniformity, accuracy, stewardship, consistency and accountability of an organization’s official shared master data assets.

IT Service Management

ITSM is the discipline of implementing, managing and delivering IT services to meet the needs of an organization. It ensures that an optimal mix of people, processes, and technology are in place to provide maximum business value.
